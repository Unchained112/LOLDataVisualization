Top Teams League

League of Legends European Championship Information Visualization

Top Teams League is a project that visualizes data from the League of Legends European Championship (LEC) from the team, player, and champion, three perspectives.

Our goal is to visualize the match data with certain game features in multi-dimension. This allows us to provide comprehensive information visualizations to offer data analysis for esports professionals and players. Besides, the visualization will act as guidance for general gamers and help game-match audiences better u nderstand the game.
